About Mapao

Discover the story behind our bi-annual magazine

Our Story

Fiction & Poetry Club, Manipur is a literary organization based in Imphal, Manipur. The club was established on 16 May 2004 under the presidentship of Naoroibam Dinamani Singh (then Regional Head of PIB, Manipur). It is registered under the Societies Registration Act with Registration No. 657/M/SR/2014.

At present, the president of the club is Dr. Hesnam Budhichandra Singh, Assistant Professor in the Department of History, Thambal Marik College, Oinam (a government college), and a Sahitya Akademi Award-winning fiction writer.

Since 2012, the club has published MAPAO, a bi-annual literary journal with ISSN 2347-9140. It is regarded as one of the leading literary journals of Manipuri literature.

What started as a small team of passionate individuals has grown into a vibrant community of writers, artists, photographers, and readers who share our vision of celebrating diverse voices and perspectives.

1

Theme Selection

Each issue begins with careful consideration of themes that reflect current cultural conversations and timeless human experiences.

3
