About the Award

The Hijam Anganghal Ningsing Mana Award is the highest literary honor bestowed by the Fiction & Poetry Club Manipur. Named after the father of modern Manipuri literature, this prestigious award recognizes outstanding contributions to Manipuri literature across various genres including poetry, fiction, drama, and literary criticism.
