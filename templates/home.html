About Fiction & Poetry Club Manipur

Established on 16 May 2004, we are a literary organization dedicated to promoting Manipuri literature and culture

Our Mission

To promote and preserve Manipuri literature through various literary activities, workshops, and publications.

Our Community

A vibrant community of writers, poets, researchers, and literature enthusiasts from Manipur and beyond.

Our Achievement

Publisher of MAPAO, a leading bi-annual literary journal with ISSN 2347-9140, recognized in Manipuri literature.

Our Activities

Engaging in various literary activities to promote Manipuri literature and culture

Writing Workshops

Regular workshops for aspiring writers to hone their skills in Manipuri literature.

Poetry Recitals

Monthly poetry recitals and literary discussions to celebrate Manipuri poetry.

Literary Seminars

Annual seminars and conferences on Manipuri literature and cultural studies.

Writing Competitions

Regular competitions to encourage new talent in Manipuri writing and poetry.

Join Our Literary Community

Be part of Manipur's premier literary organization. Connect with fellow writers, participate in workshops, and contribute to the rich tradition of Manipuri literature.
